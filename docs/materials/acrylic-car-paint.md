# Acrylic Car Paint (Spray)

Single-stage acrylic automotive spray used to add a coloured layer between clear coats on carbon fins.

## Overview
- Bonds well to scuffed or primed surfaces and flashes off quickly for thin, even coats.
- Compatible with acrylic clear coats; seal with a final clear layer after colour.

## Usage notes
- Shake the can thoroughly and apply light passes to avoid runs.
- Allow the colour coat to flash off fully before sealing with clear coat.
