# Super Glue (Cyanoacrylate)

Fast-setting adhesive for tacking lightweight parts such as plywood wedges and plastic braces.

## Overview
- Bonds quickly to wood, plastic, and acrylic; ideal for locking the wedge geometry before loading.
- Small tubes reduce waste and clogging; keep a few on hand for field repairs.

## Usage notes
- Use sparingly to avoid squeeze-out that could lift the acrylic sheet.
- Ventilate well; accelerator spray is optional but speeds up fixturing.
